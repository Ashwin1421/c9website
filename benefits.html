Benefits

Cloud Nine prides itself on providing a very attractive and highly competitive benefits package to all of its employees. We believe that best talent deserves best benefits. We take care of our employees so that they can focus on taking care of our customers. All benefits are effective on the date of hire.

Health Insurance through Aetna (PPO)

  • Cloud Nine pays for 70% of the monthly premium cost for employees and their dependents
  • Three-tier prescription plan
  • Aetna's plans also include a vision discount program through their Vision network

Dental Insurance through Aetna

  • Cloud Nine pays 70% of the monthly premium cost for employees and dependents
  • Utilizes Preferred Provider Network best discounts when in-network

Life Insurance through Aetna

  • Cloud Nine pays the premium for Basic Life Insurance at 100% of base salary (max. $50,000) through Unum-Provident for all full-time employees
  • Employees have the option to purchase additional life insurance for themselves, spouse, and dependent children
